Help

Q: How do I change a Reservation?

A: To amend or cancel a reservation, please contact the restaurant by phone to request changes

Q: How do I register for Online Reservations?

A: Just make a reservation and you are automatically registered. Once you have registered you will be entitled to a range of specials only available to those that have joined

Q: I can't make a reservation for the time I want.

A: From time to time the restaurant will have limited availability due to the popularity of certain seating times. Please place reservations in advance of the day where possible to avoid disappointment. Please also check the opening times if you are unsure whether the restaurant is opened at the time of booking.

Q: What are the benefits of using the App?

A: Making a reservation may allow access to specials only available if the reservation was made this way. It also will enable us to keep you up to date with Imagine events and other promotions. Also, the app shows the latest menu if you wish to browse what you could order when you dine in or even contact us for a delivery or takeaway order.

Q: How do I know my reservation has been accepted?

A: When you reservation is placed shortly afterwards we will issue you with a notification by SMS and also to your email address. You may also receive reminders if your reservation is made in advance of the day the reservation is for.

Q: On which mobile devices can the App be used?

The App is currently available on IPhone, iPad and all android devices

Q: Does using the App Cost anything?

A: Use of the App is free, however internet access and data charges from your mobile service provider may apply. Please contact your mobile carrier to obtain details on the cost of their services.
