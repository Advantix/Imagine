Help

Q: How do I change a Reservation?

A: Simply click on make a reservation, find an available time and provide your contact details.

Q: How do I register?

A: Just make a reservation and you are automatically registered. Once you have registered you will be entitled to a range of specials only available to those that have joined

Q: I can't make a reservation for the time I want.

A: Only periods when the restaurant is opened will be displayed. Please check the opening times if you are unsure whether the restaurant is opened at the time of booking. From time to time the restaurant will have limited availability due to the popularity of certain time slots. Please place reservations in advance of the day where possible to avoid disappointment

Q: What are the benefits of using the App?

A: Making a reservation may allow access to specials only available if the reservation was made this way. It also will enable us to keep you up to date with Imagine events and other promotions. Also, the app has our take away menu if you wish to browse and contact us for a delivery or takeaway order.

Q: How do I know my reservation has been accepted?

A: When you reservation is placed shortly afterwards we will issue you with a notification by SMS and also to your email address. You may also receive reminders if your reservation is made more than 48 hours in advance of the booking.
